OUR STORY & PROGRESS OVER THE YEARS

2006

Established FinAsialand Realty Corporation (FLRC), focusing on residential developments. This marked the company’s entry into the real estate industry.

2016

Launched FinAsialand Land Development and Construction Corp., expanding into retail and commercial property development to support business growth.

2017

Partnered with Curo Teknika I. to deliver a 300-seat office space at Citadines, beginning our involvement in office and BPO developments.

2021

Supported Concentric in opening a 70-seat office at Citadines, further strengthening our presence in the BPO sector.

2022

Established MEPANGILINAN REAL ESTATE BROKERAGE CORP. (MREB), specializing in office leasing and brokerage. Delivered office solutions for:

  • Call Centre Universe – 38 seats at Ayala 30th
  • Call Hounds GLOBAL BPO – 400 seats at Citadines
  • Digital Dolphin Asia – 30 seats at Ayala 30th

2024

Achieved significant milestones:
22,000 sqm leased office & commercial spaces
4,870 sqm built-to-suit office for INFOCOM (900 agents, turned over Aug 2024)

2025

Continued growth through partnerships:

  • Collaborations with Timezone, iCare, ASDI, JABRA, and F.A.T Foods
  • 3,652 sqm built-to-suit office for Carenet Health (480 agents, turned over Apr 2025)
